Frequently Asked Questions

Do candidates have to pay any charges for placement?

No, our job placement services are generally free of charge for candidates. We are compensated by the employer companies that we partner with to fulfill their hiring requirements. We believe in providing equal opportunity for all talent.

What kind of industries do you specialize in?

We have strong expertise and extensive client networks in IT & Software, BPO/KPO, Sales & Marketing, Banking & Finance, Retail, and Administration across the Delhi NCR region and PAN India.

How quickly can I get a job through Skillguru?

The timeline depends on your profile and actively open roles, but typically our candidates receive interview schedules within 24 to 48 hours of sharing their resume. Immediate joiners for BPO and Sales roles are sometimes placed the very same day.

Do you help with resume preparation?

Yes, we offer professional resume support to ensure your profile stands out to top employers. Just reach out to one of our consultants and we will guide you on optimizing your CV.
